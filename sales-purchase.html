Configure Sales & Purchasing

3.1 Inventory Costing

You must know the Costing Methods defined on the Item Card:

  • FIFO: First In, First Out. (Most common for distribution).
  • LIFO: Last In, First Out.
  • Specific: Tracks the exact cost of a specific Serial Number (High value items).
  • Standard: Uses a pre-defined cost. Differences are posted to "Variance" accounts (Manufacturing).

3.2 Pricing and Discounts

BC uses Sales Price Lists.

You can set prices based on:

  • Customer (Specific)
  • Customer Price Group (e.g., "Wholesale")
  • Campaign
  • Date Range
