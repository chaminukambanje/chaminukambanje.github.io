Module 3: Configure Sales and Purchasing

Inventory Costing, Prices, and Vendor/Customer setup.

3.1 Inventory Costing Methods

Defined on the Item Card. Once entries are posted, this is very hard to change.

  • FIFO (First In First Out): Standard for distribution. Oldest stock is sold first.
  • LIFO: Rarely used due to accounting rules, but available.
  • Average: Costs are weighted. Calculations can be per Item or per Item/Location/Variant.
  • Standard: Used for Manufacturing. Costs are fixed; deviations are posted to Variance accounts.

3.2 Sales Prices & Discounts

Business Central moved to a "New Sales Pricing Experience" (Feature Management).

Price Lists

You create Sales Price Lists which can apply to:

  • All Customers
  • Specific Customer
  • Customer Price Group (e.g., "Retail", "Wholesale")
  • Campaigns

Discount Types

  • Line Discount: % off specific items (e.g., Buy 10 chairs, get 5% off).
  • Invoice Discount: % off the total bill (e.g., Order over $1000 gets 2% off total).
