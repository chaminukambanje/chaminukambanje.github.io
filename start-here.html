The MB-800 Exam Profile

The Microsoft Certified: Dynamics 365 Business Central Functional Consultant Associate exam measures your ability to set up Business Central, configure financials, and manage sales/purchasing operations.

Key Details

  • Time Limit: 120 Minutes (approx. 100 mins for questions).
  • Question Count: 40-60 questions.
  • Passing Score: 700 / 1000.
  • Experience Level: Intermediate. You are expected to know "Small to Medium Business" (SMB) requirements.
Warning: Case Studies
You will likely face 1 or 2 "Case Studies" at the start or end of the exam. You cannot go back to these questions once you finish the section. Read the requirements carefully!

Question Types

Type Description Strategy
Multiple Choice Standard select 1 or select 2 correct answers. Eliminate obviously wrong answers (like "Restart the server").
Drag and Drop Match steps to a process order. Focus on the logical flow (e.g., Quote comes before Invoice).
Hotspot Click a specific area on a screenshot or diagram. Usually tests menu locations or ribbon buttons.
