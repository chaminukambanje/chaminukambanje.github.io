Exam Strategy & Overview

The Dynamics 365 Business Central Functional Consultant Associate (MB-800) exam validates your ability to configure and use the application.

Key Stats

  • Passing Score: 700 / 1000
  • Questions: 40 to 60
  • Time: 120 Minutes
Important: You cannot use the "Back" button during the Case Study sections of the exam. Make sure you answer all questions in a Case Study before clicking Next.

Question Types

You will encounter:

  1. Multiple Choice: Standard questions.
  2. Drag and Drop: Arrange steps in the correct order (e.g., "Order of the purchase process").
  3. Hotspot: Click the correct button on a screenshot (e.g., "Where do you click to post the invoice?").
