Module 4: Perform Business Operations

Managing the daily lifecycle of sales, purchasing, and banking.

4.1 The Document Lifecycle

Understanding the flow of documents is critical for the exam.

Sales Flow

  1. Sales Quote: Offer to customer (Does not affect stock).
  2. Sales Order: Confirmation (Allocates stock).
  3. Posted Sales Shipment: Removes item from inventory (affects Item Ledger).
  4. Posted Sales Invoice: Finalizes financials (affects Cust. Ledger & G/L).
Exam Tip: You can skip the "Order" step and go straight from Quote to Invoice if your process is simple. However, if you need partial shipping, you must use a Sales Order.

4.2 Banking & Journals

Payment Registration

A simplified tool to mark multiple customer invoices as "Paid" in one screen. It automatically creates and posts the lump sum to the bank account.

Bank Reconciliation

Matching the bank statement lines to your internal Bank Ledger Entries.

  • Auto-Match: BC can automatically match lines based on text/amount.
  • Transfer to GL: If a fee appears on the bank statement that isn't in BC, you can transfer it to a General Journal directly from the Rec screen.
