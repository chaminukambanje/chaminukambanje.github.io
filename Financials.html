2.1 Posting Groups

This is the most critical part of the exam. Business Central uses "Posting Groups" to link customers and items to the G/L.

Group Name Description Example
Gen. Bus. Posting Group Who we are dealing with. Domestic, EU, Export
Gen. Prod. Posting Group What we are buying/selling. Retail, Service, Raw Mat
Customer Posting Group Links to the Receivables Account. A/R Domestic

2.2 Dimensions

Dimensions allow you to analyze data without creating new G/L accounts.

  • Global Dimensions: You can only have 2. They appear everywhere and are fast to filter.
  • Shortcut Dimensions: You can have up to 6 more (Total 8). They appear on Journal lines but are harder to filter in the G/L entries.
